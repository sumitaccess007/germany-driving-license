## Point System (Punkte in Flensburg)

| Points | Reason / Type of Offence | Examples | Point Expiry |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 Point | Serious administrative offences | Speeding, parking offences, incorrect tyres | 2.5 years |
| 2 Points | Particularly serious administrative offences or offences with driving ban | Driving under influence (0.5‰ alcohol), red light violation (>1 sec or with danger), reversing on motorway | 5 years |
| 3 Points | Criminal offences involving driving disqualification | Hit and run, involuntary homicide, failure to provide assistance | 10 years |

<br><br>

## Probationary Period (Probezeit)

| Topic | Details |
|---|---|
| Normal probationary period | 2 years |
| Extended probationary period | Can be extended up to 4 years |
| A-Violation (serious offence) | Example: Ignoring STOP sign |
| B-Violation (less serious offence) | Two B-violations are treated similar to one A-violation |
| Consequence of 1 A or 2 B violations | Mandatory advanced/retraining seminar (ASF) |
| If seminar order is ignored | Driving licence can be withdrawn/revoked |
| Need to retake driving exam? | No |
| Additional fine for not attending seminar | No additional fine |
| Points added for not attending seminar | No |
| Purpose of advanced seminar | Improve considerate driving and risk awareness |
| Important Note | Attending an advanced seminar does **NOT** reduce points in the Central Register of Road Traffic Offenders |

<br><br>

## Important Learning Points

| Question | Correct Understanding |
|---|---|
| What should be achieved by attending an advanced seminar during probationary period? | More considerate driving attitude and improved risk awareness |
| Does attending ASF reduce points? | No |
| What happens if a learner driver ignores the official order to attend the seminar? | Driving licence may be withdrawn |
